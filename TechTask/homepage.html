Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect from my first training session?

Your first session includes a comprehensive fitness assessment, goal setting discussion, and movement screening. We'll evaluate your current fitness level, discuss your objectives, and create a personalized training plan. You'll also learn proper form for basic exercises and get familiar with our training philosophy.

How often should I train to see results?

For optimal results, we recommend 2-3 training sessions per week. This frequency allows for proper recovery while maintaining consistent progress. Beginners may start with 2 sessions weekly, while more experienced individuals can benefit from 3-4 sessions. Your trainer will customize the frequency based on your goals and recovery capacity.

Do you provide nutritional guidance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive nutritional coaching as part of our training programs. Our certified trainers provide meal planning guidance, macro tracking support, and lifestyle nutrition advice. We focus on sustainable eating habits that complement your fitness goals and fit your lifestyle.

What equipment do you have available?

Our studio features state-of-the-art equipment including free weights, resistance machines, functional training tools, cardio equipment, and specialized apparatus for rehabilitation and injury prevention. We also have kettlebells, battle ropes, suspension trainers, and Olympic lifting platforms.

Can you help with injury recovery?

Our trainers are experienced in working with clients recovering from injuries. We collaborate with physical therapists and healthcare providers to ensure safe, effective rehabilitation. All programs are adapted to your specific limitations and progressively designed to restore strength and mobility.

What are your cancellation policies?

We require 24-hour notice for session cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be charged as a completed session. Emergency situations are handled on a case-by-case basis. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your busy lifestyle.
